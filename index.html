Introduction

This specification describes a Linked Data vocabulary for asserting Verifiable Credentials related to business banking, such as a Verified Person, Verified Organization and Verified Relationship.

Use Cases and Requirements

The following use cases outline a number of key scenarios that readers might find useful:

  • Verified Person (for the purposes of business banking)
  • Verified Organization
  • Verified Relationship

Terminology

The ISED Business Banking Vocabulary

This vocabulary assumes all terms specified in the base Verifiable Credentials [[VC-DATA-MODEL]] context. In addition, the following classes are available for specifying information related to residency and citizenship status.

Class Description
VerifiedPerson Specifies that the subject of the credential is person verified by the issuer of this credential.
VerifiedOrganization Specifies that the credential represents a government verified organization.
VerifiedRelationship Specifies that the subject of the credential is related to the organization.

This document also specifies the following terms for expressing organizational relationships:

Term Description
birthCountry The country in which a individual was born.
birthDate The date on which a individual was born.
familyName The name of the family with which the individual identifies.
gender The gender of the individual.
givenName The non-family name with which the individual identifies.
